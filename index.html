Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Dublesh.

🔍
Are my PDF files uploaded to a server?

No. Dublesh processes all files entirely in your browser using JavaScript. Your files never leave your device and are never sent to any server, cloud, or third party — not even to us.

Is Dublesh safe for confidential documents?

Yes. Everything runs locally in your browser — ideal for contracts, medical records, and financial statements. No file data ever leaves your device. We have no servers that receive your PDFs.

How does Dublesh protect my privacy?

Your files are processed in your browser's memory using pdf-lib and PDF.js — both open-source, MIT-licensed libraries. We use Plausible for cookie-free analytics that collects no personal data. Our CSP header blocks all unauthorised external requests.

Does Dublesh use cookies or tracking?

No cookies. Dublesh uses Plausible — a privacy-first analytics tool that collects no personal data and sets no cookies. We track only anonymous page-level metrics to understand which tools are most useful.

How does PDF encryption work in Dublesh?

Protect PDF applies real AES-128 encryption using pdf-lib entirely in your browser. Set a password and choose permissions (printing, copying, annotations). The encrypted file requires the password to open in any PDF reader — no server involved.

Are Crop PDF and Add Image also private?

Absolutely. Like all 51 tools, Crop PDF and Add Image process everything in your browser using pdf-lib. No image or PDF data is ever transmitted anywhere. The result downloads directly from your browser's memory.

Is Dublesh really free to use?

Yes — completely free. All 51 tools are available with no subscription, no account, and no usage limits. No watermarks are added to output files. Support the project via Ko-fi if it's helpful.

How do I compress a PDF to under 1 MB?

Open Compress PDF and drag the quality slider to 40–60%. Enable "Strip metadata" for extra savings. For Aadhaar or government portal uploads, tap the 🇮🇳 Aadhaar Preset button — it sets the exact settings needed in one click.

How do I crop page margins in a PDF?

Use Crop PDF. Enter margin values in millimetres for left, right, top, and bottom. Choose a quick preset (5mm, 10mm, 15mm) or use the Aadhaar preset for standard government document dimensions. Apply to all pages or odd/even only.

How do I add a logo or image to a PDF?

Use Add Image to PDF. Upload your PDF and choose a PNG, JPG or WebP image. Set width, height and X/Y position in mm, choose opacity, and apply to all pages, first page, or last page. Perfect for company logos, stamps, or graphic watermarks.

Can I merge multiple PDF files at once?

Yes. Merge PDF lets you drop multiple files and combine them in any order — sequential, interleaved, or reversed. Drag to reorder files before merging. The result downloads as a single PDF immediately.

Does Dublesh PDF to Excel produce real .xlsx files?

Yes. Dublesh uses SheetJS to generate genuine .xlsx files that open in Excel, Google Sheets, LibreOffice, and Numbers. You can also export as CSV, TSV, or JSON with smart table detection across multiple sheets.

Do Dublesh AI tools upload my PDF to a server?

No. Text is extracted from your PDF locally in your browser, then sent directly to the Anthropic Claude API using your own API key. No PDF binary data is ever transmitted. Dublesh is not in the data path.

Where do I get a free Anthropic API key?

Visit console.anthropic.com, create a free account, and go to API Keys → Create Key. New accounts receive free credits — enough for ~2,000 PDF summaries. Your key is stored in your browser only.

What can AI Summarize do?

AI Summarize uses Claude to create structured summaries. Choose from 6 styles: Balanced, Bullet points, Executive summary, Detailed analysis, Plain language (ELI5), or Legal/technical. Works on multi-page PDFs up to 50 MB.

How many languages does AI Translate support?

27 languages: Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Hindi, Arabic, Chinese (Simplified and Traditional), Japanese, Korean, Russian, Turkish, Polish, Swedish, and more. Choose Faithful, Natural, Formal, or Casual style.

Is my API key stored securely?

Your Anthropic API key is saved in your browser's localStorage (persisted) or sessionStorage (session-only). It is never sent to Dublesh servers — because there are none. Clear it at any time from the AI tool's key field.

How does Dublesh AI compare to Adobe's AI?

Dublesh's AI tools use Claude with your own API key — no extra subscription. Adobe's AI Acrobat costs $25+/month. Dublesh AI Q&A and Translate are not available in Adobe's free tier. Your data stays private with Dublesh.

Can I use Dublesh on iPhone or iPad?

Yes. Dublesh is fully responsive and works in Safari on iPhone and iPad. Tap "Share → Add to Home Screen" to install it as a PWA that works offline. All 51 tools are touch-optimised with large tap targets and swipe support.

Does Dublesh work on Android?

Yes. Works in Chrome on Android. Tap the browser menu → "Add to Home Screen" or "Install App" to install the PWA. All tools support touch, drag-and-drop, and file sharing from Android's share sheet.

Does Dublesh work offline?

Yes. Dublesh is a Progressive Web App. After first visit, all files cache locally — use all 51 tools offline on a plane or without internet. AI tools need connectivity for the Anthropic API only.

What is the maximum PDF file size?

Up to 150 MB depending on device RAM. Desktop browsers (Chrome or Firefox) handle large files best. On mobile, files above 50 MB may be slow. Use Compress PDF first to reduce large files before further processing.

Which browsers does Dublesh support?

All modern browsers: Chrome 90+, Firefox 90+, Safari 15+, Edge 90+, and Samsung Internet. JavaScript must be enabled. For best performance on desktop, Chrome or Firefox is recommended.

Does Dublesh work on Windows, Mac and Linux?

Yes — on any OS with a modern browser. No installation needed on desktop. Works identically on Windows, macOS, Linux, and ChromeOS. Install as a PWA from the browser for a native-app experience.

How do I add a signature to a PDF?

Use Sign PDF. Draw with mouse or finger, type your name in a handwriting font, or upload a signature photo. Position it anywhere on any page, optionally add a date stamp, then download. Everything runs locally — nothing is transmitted.

How do I password-protect a PDF?

Use Protect PDF. Upload, enter a password, and set permissions — allow or block printing, copying, and annotations. Dublesh applies real AES-128 encryption via pdf-lib in your browser. The result requires the password in any PDF reader.

Can I merge password-protected PDFs?

Yes — first use Unlock PDF to remove the password, then Merge PDF to combine. Both steps happen entirely in your browser with no uploads.

Can I save my favourite tools?

Yes. Click the heart (♡) on any tool card to pin it to Favourites. Pinned tools appear in a dedicated Favourites tab for instant access. Saved in localStorage and persist across visits.

What keyboard shortcuts does Dublesh have?

Press / or ⌘K for search, Esc to close modals, ? for shortcut panel, D to toggle dark mode, H for history, ← → to navigate carousels, and 1 to show all tools.

Does Dublesh track my operation history?

Yes. Every successful operation is saved locally (up to 50 entries). Press H to open the history panel. On return visits, "Continue where you left off" shows your 3 most recent tools above the grid.

How does Dublesh compare to SmallPDF or ILovePDF?

Dublesh never uploads files. SmallPDF limits free users to 2 tasks/day and adds watermarks. ILovePDF uploads files and limits free use similarly. Dublesh is completely free, includes AI tools, works offline, and has no limits.

What is the best free PDF tool in India?

Dublesh is built by Indian indie developer Nilesh (@mydublesh) — completely free, no uploads. The Compress tool includes an Aadhaar Preset that compresses to under 2 MB for UIDAI and government portal uploads in one click.

Who built Dublesh?

Built by Nilesh under the Dublesh brand — an Indian indie developer. Follow on X (@mydublesh), connect on LinkedIn, or support at Ko-fi.

How do I share a tool link with pre-set settings?

Open any tool and click "↗ Share" in the modal header. The shareable URL includes your current settings — e.g. dublesh.com/#compress?quality=60 — so colleagues open the tool with identical configuration.

Can I search tools using synonyms?

Yes. The search understands 50+ synonyms — try "combine" for Merge, "shrink" for Compress, "password" for Protect, "esign" for Sign, "bw" for Grayscale, "trim" for Crop, "stamp" for Add Image, or "translate" for AI Translate.

Can I convert a PDF to Word for free?

Yes. PDF to Word extracts text and produces a .doc file compatible with Word, Google Docs, and LibreOffice. Best for text-heavy PDFs — complex layouts and images may not transfer perfectly. Scanned PDFs need OCR (coming soon).
